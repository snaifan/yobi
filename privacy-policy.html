Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At AT Fields, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and follow best practices for data protection.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with AT Fields:

A. Information You Provide Directly

When you subscribe to our newsletter or download free resources:

  • Email address (required)
  • First name (optional, to personalize communications)

When you join the Private Group (paid subscription):

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Gumroad - we do NOT store credit card details)
  • Telegram username (to grant access to private channel)

When you contact us:

  • Name and email address
  • Message content and any information you choose to share
  • Response correspondence

In plain English: We only collect what you voluntarily give us - your email when you subscribe, your payment details when you purchase (handled securely by Gumroad), and any info you share when contacting us.

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information:

Usage Data:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version (e.g., Chrome, Safari)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Operating system (Windows, Mac, iOS, Android)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referral source (how you found us - search, social media, direct link)
  • Date and time of visit

Cookies and Similar Technologies:

We use cookies to improve your experience. See the Cookies section below for details.

C. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your trading account credentials or broker information
  • Payment card details (handled by Gumroad)
  • Sensitive personal data (race, religion, health, political views)
  • Location data beyond general country/city (via IP)
  • Biometric data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

A. To Provide Our Services

  • Deliver content you requested (free guide, newsletter, blog updates)
  • Grant access to paid services (Private Group, trading plan PDF)
  • Send trade alerts and weekly prep (for Private Group members)
  • Facilitate Q&A sessions and community interactions
  • Process subscriptions and payments (via Gumroad)

B. To Communicate With You

  • Send weekly newsletters (if you subscribed)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Notify you of service changes (Terms, Privacy Policy updates)
  • Send transactional emails (payment confirmations, access links)
  • Share educational content you requested

Marketing Emails: We will only send you promotional emails if you explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email.

C. To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze website usage to improve user experience
  • Understand which content is most valuable to readers
  • Test new features and optimize performance
  • Monitor and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Ensure technical stability and security

D. For Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations under Kenyan law
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Respond to law enforcement requests (when legally required)

Legal Basis (Kenya Data Protection Act): We process your data based on: (1) Your consent, (2) Performance of our contract with you, (3) Our legitimate business interests, and (4) Legal obligations.

5. Your Data Protection Rights (Kenya Data Protection Act)

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Your Rights:

1. Right to Be Informed

You have the right to know what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it (this Privacy Policy).

2. Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We'll provide this within 30 days.

3. Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data we hold about you.

4. Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

5. Right to Object

You can object to processing of your data for marketing purposes or based on legitimate interests.

6. Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format to transfer to another service.

7. Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time (e.g., unsubscribe from emails).

How to Exercise Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ebosobrain@gmail.com with:

  • Your full name and email address
  • Specific right you're exercising
  • Details of your request

We'll respond within 30 days and verify your identity before processing your request.

6. How We Protect Your Data

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

Security Measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our website uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Secure Payment Processing: Payment data is handled by Gumroad (PCI DSS compliant) - we never store credit card details
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data - only authorized personnel
  • Regular Updates: We keep software and systems updated with latest security patches
  • Third-Party Vetting: We only work with reputable service providers with strong security practices

Important Disclaimer: While we implement strong security measures, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we commit to protecting your data to the best of our ability and notifying you of any breaches as required by law.

Data Retention:

  • Active subscribers: We retain data while your subscription is active
  • After cancellation: Data retained for 12 months for legal/tax purposes, then deleted
  • Newsletter subscribers: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Contact inquiries: Retained for 2 years for record-keeping

7. International Data Transfers

AT Fields operates from Kenya, but some of our third-party service providers (Beehiiv, Gumroad, Google) may store data on servers outside Kenya.

When data is transferred internationally, we ensure:

  • Service providers comply with data protection standards equivalent to the Kenya Data Protection Act
  • Appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • Your rights remain protected regardless of where data is stored

8. Children's Privacy

AT Fields services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18.

If you are under 18:

  • Do not register for our services
  • Do not provide any personal information
  • Ask a parent or guardian for permission before using our website

If you are a parent/guardian:

If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at ebosobrain@gmail.com and we will delete it.

Trading Education Context: Our content is educational but involves financial concepts that may not be suitable for minors. Parental supervision is strongly recommended.
